What inspires you to learn?

I feel like the motivation behind me wanting to learn is improving myself so i can become a better person than i was the day before. Another reason behind my inspiration is to be more independent because by learning skills i would be able to perform them myself without assistances. In addition, by improving myself i would be able to help other people who need help. Overall, my inspiration behind learning is about improving my capabilities and helping others in the process.

How it impacts you?

The inspiration to learn impacts me personally, by helping my personal growth because it allowing me to enjoy the learning process of new things. It also impacts me by making me more curious and helps me develop profound thoughts. Futhermore, it helps me look at challenges in a more positive manner, which i feel helps me push through harder task that other people may find diffcult. Altogether, the imspiration to learn heavily influences, how i view obstacles and it helps my personal growth.
